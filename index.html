Frequently Asked Questions

How do I find a sitter?

Simply sign up, enter your location, and browse pet parents or professional sitters nearby.

Are sitters verified?

Yes. Every sitter on Peho undergoes verification for identity and pet care experience.

Is Peho free to join?

Yes! You only pay for the services you choose.

What if my pet has special needs?

You can mention all requirements in your profile, and Peho will match you with suitable sitters.
